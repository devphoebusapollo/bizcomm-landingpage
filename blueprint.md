
# Blueprint: English Communication for the Workplace Landing Page

## Overview

This document outlines the plan for creating a modern, visually appealing, and effective landing page for an online course titled "Mastering English Communication for the Workplace." The goal is to attract and convert professionals looking to enhance their workplace communication skills.

## Design and Features

*   **Theme:** A soft, minimalist, and modern design with high-contrast alternating section backgrounds.
*   **Color Palette:** A warm beige (`#DDD0C8`) and a dark gray (`#323232`) used for alternating section backgrounds and a consistent footer.
*   **Typography:** "Poppins" for all text for a clean, modern, and highly readable look.
*   **Layout:** Full-width sections with centered content to create a modern, spacious feel.
*   **Header:** A sticky beige header for persistent navigation, with a responsive hamburger menu for mobile and tablet.
*   **Animations:** Smooth scrolling, subtle fade-in, smooth accordion transitions, and gentle hover effects.
*   **Sections:**
    *   Hero: A compelling introduction to the course.
    *   The Story: A narrative section on the importance of workplace communication, presented as centered text.
    *   Benefits: An infographic visually representing the key outcomes of the course.
    *   Call to Action (CTA): A dedicated section to encourage enrollment.
    *   Curriculum: An interactive carousel of redesigned module cards with on-scroll auto-play.
    *   Why Join The Program: A redesigned, modern section that highlights the key benefits of the program in a visually appealing way.
    *   Gallery: An animated gallery with six vertically-aligned sample class images. Gallery card overlays will include a title and a descriptive paragraph.
    *   Testimonials: An auto-scrolling carousel of testimonial cards with on-scroll auto-play, functional on all screen sizes.
    *   FAQ: Answers to common questions.
*   **Interactive Elements:**
    *   "Enroll Now" buttons with icons, leading to a signup modal.
    *   Accordion-style FAQ for easy browsing.
